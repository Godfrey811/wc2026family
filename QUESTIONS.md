Fill in **all 11** before the first kickoff (**11 June, 18:00 BST**). One entry per person.

📊 **Read the [stats guide](https://godfrey811.github.io/wc2026family/guide.html) first** -
especially: 2026 has **104 matches, not 64**, so scale every total up ~1.6x.

**Scoring - points tote (pari-mutuel):** each question has a **points pot** (100 unless the
**Pts** column says otherwise). Everyone who gets a question right **splits its pot equally** -
right on your own = the whole pot, shared with others = an equal slice. So being right
*and contrarian* wins. Closest-number questions: nearest value wins (ties split). Points add
up across all questions into one leaderboard, prizes to the top.
